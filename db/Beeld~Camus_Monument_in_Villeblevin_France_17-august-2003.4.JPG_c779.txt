[edit] Summary

   The bronze plaque on the monument to the French writer and philosopher
   Albert Camus (1913-1960), built in the small town of Villeblevin
   (France) where he found death in a car crash on January 4, 1960. The
   plaque reads: "From the Yonne area's local council, in tribute to the
   writer Albert Camus who was watched over in the Villeblevin town hall
   in the night of the 4th to the 5th of January 1960". Date of picture:
   August 17, 2003.
