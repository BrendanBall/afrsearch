   Ancient Chinese Wedding attire

   These two cartoonish statues depict the ancient Chinese wedding gowns.
   Note the red theme which symbolizes happiness and good fortune. No one
   dresses like the groom on the right anymore. But a slight variation of
   the bride's dress is still in use in some Chinese wedding nowadays.

   In a modern Chinese wedding in Hong Kong, the bride would wear white
   Western wedding gown to walk down the aisle in church. Then in the
   wedding banquet in the evening, the bride starts with a red Chinese
   wedding gown like the one shown on the left of this picture. A tea
   ceremony to honor and receive gift from the older generation is
   performed in the traditional gown. Later she changes into the white
   wedding gown to cut the wedding cake. Towards the last third of the
   banquet, she changes into a Chinese qipao to say thanks to the guests
   as they leave the banquet.

   Note that different regions in China have different wedding tradition.
