[edit] Summary

   Photo of Japan playing Tonga at Honjo stadium in Kitakyushu city,
   Fukuoka prefecture, Japan on June 4, 2006 in the inaugural IRB Pacific
   5 Nations 2006.　The stadium was almost full with 8,100 spectators, but
   Japan lost 16-57. It was the first time in 22 years that the Japan
   national side had played in Kyushu. (Photo taken by uploader, Ian
   Ruxton)
