[edit] Summary

   This is a photo of the Scandinavian memorial in honour of those of the
   Scandinavian volunteer corps who gallantly fought the British Imperial
   forces at the Battle of Magersfontein near Kimberley.

   The text is in (old ?) Swedish language and translates like this:
     * De kunde icke vika: They could not retreat
     * Blott falla kunde de: They could only fall
     * Til minne om här stupade skandinaver: In memory of Scandinavians
       killed here.

   --Sir48 18:17, 18 January 2006 (UTC)
