Wikipediabespreking:Wat is 'n artikel


   Ek vermoet die skakel vanaf die statistiekbladsy veronderstel dat
   hierdie artikel gaan verduidelik wat as artikels getel word deur die
   sagteware en wat nie. Ek dink hierdie is die relevante paragraaf:

          The automatic definition used by the software at
          Special:Statistics is: any page that is in the article
          namespace, is not a redirect page and contains at least one wiki
          link. The statistics software currently has no method of
          detecting disambiguation pages, however; nor does it disregard
          stubs (but in any case many articles tagged as stubs are quite
          substantial) or stublists (lists templates with little or no
          content).

   --Alias 06:46, 16 Mei 2006 (UTC)
